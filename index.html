Hello and welcome, in 2016 few people urged me to stop waiting for someone else and create local hub for all the people who are interested in PostgreSQL and are around Tallinn. It seemed great idea, Margus and Kaarel offered to help me and so 3 of us started.

While we are not too active, most questions get answered in facebook within hours. Several companies have offered us help and we can already thank Cybertec, Toggl, TransferWise, Taxify and Monese for help and hospitality. Share the passion, tell us stories about your bad and good PostgreSQL experiences and we will do our best to keep events coming.

Ervin.
